# Tripitaka (Pali Canon)

The **Tripitaka**, or **Pali Canon**, is the principal scripture of **Theravāda Buddhism**, traditionally organized into three "baskets":

## Three Baskets
1. **Vinaya Pitaka** — monastic rules and discipline.  
2. **Sutta Pitaka** — discourses and teachings of the Buddha.  
3. **Abhidhamma Pitaka** — systematic philosophical and psychological analysis.

## Language and Role
- Preserved in **Pali**; foundational for Theravāda doctrine and practice; originally transmitted orally.

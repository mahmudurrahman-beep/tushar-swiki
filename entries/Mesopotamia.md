## Geography
- Located in the **Fertile Crescent** of Southwest Asia.  
- Rivers: **Tigris** and **Euphrates** provided irrigation and fertile soil.  
- Modern countries: Iraq, Syria, Turkey, Iran.  

## Early Civilizations
- **Sumerians (c. 4500–1900 BCE):** Invented cuneiform writing, built city-states like Ur and Uruk.  
- **Akkadians (c. 2334–2154 BCE):** First empire under Sargon of Akkad.  
- **Babylonians (c. 1894–539 BCE):** Famous for Hammurabi’s Code and the Hanging Gardens.  
- **Assyrians (c. 2500–609 BCE):** Powerful military empire with Nineveh as capital.  

## Achievements
- **Writing:** Cuneiform script, earliest known writing system.  
- **Law:** Hammurabi’s Code (first written legal code).  
- **Mathematics & Astronomy:** Base-60 system (influenced timekeeping), early star charts.  
- **Architecture:** Ziggurats (step temples), canals, city walls.  

## Legacy
- Known as the **“Cradle of Civilization”**.  
- Influenced later cultures in law, science, and governance.  
- Many Mesopotamian myths (like *Epic of Gilgamesh*) shaped literature and religion.

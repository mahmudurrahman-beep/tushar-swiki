## Origins
- Created by **Sony executive Ken Kutaragi**.  
- First console released in **Japan on December 3, 1994**, and worldwide in 1995.  
- The original PlayStation became the **first console to ship over 100 million units**.  

## Console Generations
- **PlayStation (PS1)** – 1994, revolutionized 3D gaming.  
- **PlayStation 2 (PS2)** – 2000, best-selling console ever (~155 million units).  
- **PlayStation 3 (PS3)** – 2006, introduced Blu-ray and online gaming.  
- **PlayStation 4 (PS4)** – 2013, focused on social gaming and streaming.  
- **PlayStation 5 (PS5)** – 2020, features ultra-fast SSD, ray tracing, and DualSense controller.  

## Handhelds & Other Products
- **PlayStation Portable (PSP)** – 2004, successful handheld.  
- **PlayStation Vita** – 2011, advanced handheld but less commercially successful.  
- **PlayStation VR** – virtual reality headset launched in 2016.  
- **PlayStation Network (PSN)** – online gaming, digital store, subscriptions (PS Plus).  

## Cultural Impact
- Iconic franchises: **Final Fantasy, Metal Gear Solid, Gran Turismo, God of War, Uncharted, The Last of Us**.  
- PlayStation helped shape modern gaming culture, esports, and digital entertainment.  
- Slogan: **“Play Has No Limits.”**  

## Legacy
PlayStation is not just a console brand but a **multimedia ecosystem**, spanning games, films, TV, and online services. It remains a leader in the gaming industry with over **500 million consoles sold worldwide** across all generations.

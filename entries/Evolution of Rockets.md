# Evolution of Rockets

From ancient gunpowder devices to modern launch vehicles and reusable systems.

## Early Origins
- **China:** fire arrows and early solid-fuel rockets.  
- Developments in multi-stage concepts and rocketry theory (Tsiolkovsky, Goddard, Oberth).

## 20th Century
- **1926:** First liquid-fueled rocket (Robert Goddard).  
- **WWII:** V-2 rocket (Germany).  
- **Space Age:** Sputnik (1957), human spaceflight, Saturn V and lunar missions.

## Modern Developments
- Reusable rockets (SpaceX, Blue Origin), international launch capabilities, and research into advanced propulsion for deep-space missions.

# Bolshevik Revolution

The Bolshevik Revolution, also known as the October Revolution of 1917, was a pivotal event in Russian history that led to the establishment of the Soviet Union.

## Causes of the Revolution

1. **Economic Inequality**: Severe class divisions between aristocracy and peasants
2. **World War I Impact**: Massive casualties and economic strain
3. **Tsarist Autocracy**: Nicholas II's ineffective rule and political repression
4. **Food Shortages**: Widespread famine and inflation

## Key Events in Chronological Order

1. **February Revolution (1917)**: Overthrow of Tsar Nicholas II
2. **April Theses**: Lenin's return and "Peace, Land, Bread" slogan
3. **July Days**: Failed Bolshevik uprising
4. **October Revolution**: Storming of the Winter Palace
5. **Treaty of Brest-Litovsk (1918)**: Russia exits WWI

## Major Figures

* **Vladimir Lenin** - Leader of the Bolshevik Party
* **Leon Trotsky** - Organizer of the Red Army
* **Joseph Stalin** - People's Commissar for Nationalities
* **Alexandra Kollontai** - People's Commissar for Social Welfare
* **Felix Dzerzhinsky** - Founder of the Cheka (secret police)

## Bolshevik Policies Implemented

- Decree on Peace: Immediate withdrawal from WWI
- Decree on Land: Redistribution to peasants
- Workers' Control of Factories
- Nationalization of Banks and Industry
- Separation of Church and State

## Impact on Russian Society

### Immediate Effects:
* End of Romanov dynasty (execution in 1918)
* Establishment of the Russian Soviet Republic
* Beginning of the Russian Civil War (1917-1923)

### Long-term Consequences:
- Creation of the USSR (1922)
- Implementation of planned economy
- Spread of communist ideology globally
- Cold War tensions with the West

## Important Dates Timeline

1. **1917, March 8** - International Women's Day protests begin
2. **1917, March 15** - Tsar Nicholas II abdicates
3. **1917, April 16** - Lenin returns from exile
4. **1917, November 7** - Bolsheviks seize power (October 25 by Julian calendar)
5. **1918, March 3** - Treaty of Brest-Litovsk signed

## Revolution Slogans

> "Peace, Land, and Bread!"  
> "All Power to the Soviets!"  
> "Workers of the world, unite!"

## Key Documents

* **April Theses** - Lenin's revolutionary program
* **Decree on Peace** - Call for immediate peace
* **Decree on Land** - Land redistribution manifesto
* **Declaration of the Rights of the Working and Exploited People**

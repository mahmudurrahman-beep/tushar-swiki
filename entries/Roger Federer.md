## Early Life
- Born in **Basel, Switzerland**.  
- Began playing tennis at a young age and quickly rose through junior ranks.  
- Turned professional in **1998**.  

## Career Achievements
- **Grand Slam Titles (20 total):**  
  - Australian Open: 6  
  - French Open: 1  
  - Wimbledon: 8  
  - US Open: 5  
- **ATP Tour Titles:** 103 (second most in the Open Era).  
- **Olympics:** Gold medal in doubles (2008), silver in singles (2012).  
- **World No. 1:** Held the top ranking for **310 weeks**, including a record 237 consecutive weeks.  

## Playing Style
- Known for his **graceful movement, versatile shot-making, and one-handed backhand**.  
- Famous for his rivalries with **Rafael Nadal** and **Novak Djokovic**, which defined an era of tennis.  

## Retirement
- Announced retirement in **September 2022** at age 41.  
- Played his final professional match at the **Laver Cup**, partnering with Nadal in doubles.  

## Legacy
- Widely regarded as one of the greatest athletes of all time.  
- Admired for his sportsmanship, elegance, and contributions to tennis.  
- Inducted into the **International Tennis Hall of Fame in 2025**.

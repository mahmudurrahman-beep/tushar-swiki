## Rise to Power
- In **2014**, captured **Mosul** and declared a **caliphate** under Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi.  
- Controlled territory the size of Britain across Iraq and Syria.  
- Funded operations through oil smuggling, extortion, and looting.  

## Ideology
- Salafi-jihadist, seeking to establish a global Islamic caliphate.  
- Enforced strict Sharia law.  
- Targeted Shiites, Yazidis, Christians, and other minorities.  

## Global Impact
- Responsible for mass killings, beheadings, and terrorist attacks worldwide.  
- Destroyed ancient sites like **Palmyra**.  
- Inspired affiliates in Africa (ISWAP, ISIS-K), Asia, and beyond.  

## Decline
- Coalition forces and local militias recaptured ISIS territory by **2019**.  
- Leader Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi killed in **2019 U.S. raid**.  
- Today, ISIS operates as an **insurgency** and through global branches.  

## Legacy
- Left behind humanitarian crises in Iraq and Syria.  
- Changed global counterterrorism strategies.  
- Continues to pose a threat through decentralized networks.

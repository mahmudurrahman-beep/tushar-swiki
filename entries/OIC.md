# Organisation of Islamic Cooperation (OIC)

## Key Facts
- **Founded:** 25 September 1969, Rabat, Morocco.  
- **Headquarters:** Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.  
- **Members:** 57 states (spanning Asia, Africa, Middle East, and South America).  
- **Languages:** Arabic, English, French.  
- **Secretary-General (2025):** Hissein Brahim Taha.  

## Objectives
- Safeguard and protect the interests of the Muslim world.  
- Promote international peace and harmony.  
- Support Palestinian rights and defend Al-Quds (Jerusalem).  
- Encourage economic, social, cultural, and scientific cooperation.  

## Institutions
- **Islamic Summit:** Highest decision-making body (heads of state).  
- **Council of Foreign Ministers:** Annual policy-setting meetings.  
- **General Secretariat:** Administrative body in Jeddah.  
- Specialized agencies: Islamic Development Bank, Islamic Solidarity Fund, etc.  

## Global Role
- Represents **1.8 billion Muslims worldwide**.  
- Engages in diplomacy, humanitarian aid, and conflict resolution.  
- Advocates for Muslim minorities and addresses Islamophobia.
